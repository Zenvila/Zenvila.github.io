## The Challenge

Real estate investment decisions lack data-driven insights. Investors need predictive models to assess ROI and risk before committing capital.

## The Solution

Created a platform that scrapes 90,000+ property listings and uses machine learning to predict ROI, price trends, and investment scores.

## Key Achievement

Helped identify high-value investment opportunities with 85% accuracy, combining location data, market trends, and external factors.

## Technologies Used

Python, Web Scraping (Playwright), Machine Learning, Data Analysis, FastAPI
